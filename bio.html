About Fatime

Bridging science, data, and storytelling.

In a world where the future of humanity is inseparable from the future of our cities, Fatime Barbara stands as a visionary connector.

Dr. Fatime Barbara Hegyi is a leading voice on the future of cities — equipping individuals and institutions to reimagine the places we live, work, and thrive.

Her book, The Future of Cities: A Data-Driven Approach to Tackling Grand Challenges and Building Sustainable, Resilient Communities, offers a blueprint for navigating complexity — providing city leaders, policymakers, and organisations with practical frameworks to drive measurable progress.

Renowned for her engaging, insight-rich presentations, Fatime translates complex urban challenges into actionable strategies. Her keynotes and workshops inspire audiences across government, academia, and business — from mayors seeking to drive social progress to corporate innovators aligning strategy with sustainability and societal impact.

Beyond her speaking engagements, Fatime leads initiatives that integrate data, design, and dialogue to reshape the way cities learn and evolve. Her work emphasises the interconnectedness of urban systems — health, education, mobility, technology, and governance — showing how political will can be treated as a renewable resource when guided by evidence and shared purpose.

Speech topics

Five talks. Shaping the cities of tomorrow.

Each topic emerges from the research and the book — adapted to the audience, the context, and the moment. From keynote stages to closed-door policy roundtables.

01
Data · Analytics · Smart cities

Decoding the Urban DNA

How data is building smarter, more human-centered cities.

In the age of big data, cities are living organisms of information. Fatime unlocks the secrets of "Decoding the Urban DNA," revealing how data analytics can revolutionise urban planning and management.

Learn how to harness the power of data to optimise infrastructure, enhance public services, create personalised urban experiences, and improve the lives of citizens. Discover how to transform raw data into actionable insights that drive smarter, more efficient, and more human-centered cities.

Adapts for
Policymakers seeking evidence-based decision frameworks · Corporate leaders exploring data-driven urban strategy · Smart-city summits · Tech & analytics events · Public-sector innovation conferences
02
Business · Innovation · Strategy

Urban Innovation

Data-driven strategies for corporate growth and social impact.

The cities of the future will be shaped by those who embrace innovation. Fatime presents a forward-looking perspective on how corporations can become key players in building the city of tomorrow.

Discover how data reveals emerging urban trends and unmet needs, creating opportunities for businesses to develop innovative solutions. Explore strategies for fostering collaboration between businesses, governments, and communities to create a more sustainable, resilient, and inclusive urban future.

Adapts for
Corporate offsites & leadership retreats · ESG & sustainability programmes at scale · Boardroom strategy sessions · Industry summits · Cross-sector partnership convenings
03
Sustainability · Climate · Urban design

The Greenprint Revolution

Envisioning and building the sustainable city of tomorrow.

The climate crisis demands a radical shift in how we design and manage our cities. Fatime's Greenprint Revolution offers a visionary framework for creating truly sustainable urban environments.

Discover how to leverage data-driven insights and empower community-led initiatives to build cities that meet the needs of present and future generations. Learn how to create urban spaces that are models of ecological balance, social equity, and long-term prosperity — a lasting green legacy.

Adapts for
Policymakers shaping climate & environmental strategy · Corporate ESG programmes & sustainability summits · Mayor convenings · Climate conferences · University environmental policy programmes
04
Health · Well-being · Urban planning

Cities and Health

How healthier people create healthier cities.

The future of health walks the streets we walk, savours the food we can reach, breathes the air of our green spaces. It's not just about medicine — it's about the city itself.

Fatime explores the link between urban design and human well-being. Learn how to engineer cities that promote physical and mental health through accessible green spaces, active transportation, and community-focused initiatives. Discover how healthier citizens contribute to a more vibrant and resilient urban fabric.

Adapts for
Health-system policy bodies & public-health convenings · Corporate wellness & insurance summits · Hospital networks · Wellness conferences · Cross-sector health-and-cities programmes
05
Education · Innovation · Lifelong learning

The Urban Learning Lab

Data-driven education for urban transformation.

Education is the key to unlocking human potential and building thriving cities. Fatime presents a data-driven approach to transforming urban spaces into dynamic "Learning Labs," fostering lifelong learning and innovation.

Explore how data insights inform strategies that fuel well-being, expand opportunities, improve health outcomes, and empower individuals to contribute to a thriving society. Building cities where every street corner is a potential classroom creates a ripple effect of positive change.

Adapts for
University & academic leadership retreats · Education policymakers shaping system-level reform · EdTech conferences · Lifelong-learning summits · Foundations & philanthropy convenings
