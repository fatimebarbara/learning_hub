A Diagnostic for Urban Intelligence

Future of Cities Assessment Tool

Turning complexity into clarity — a new way to see how your city learns, adapts, and progresses.

The Assessment Tool translates the framework of The Future of Cities into an interactive diagnostic for urban intelligence. It evaluates performance across six dimensions — sustainability, health, education, inclusion, security, and innovation — and reveals your city's intelligence profile.

🌱 EmergentBuilding the foundations of collaboration and data-driven insight.
💡 InnovatorExperimenting with new models of participation and technology.
🤝 IntegratorAligning systems for coherence and shared intelligence.
🌍 RegenerativeLeading through resilience, empathy, and long-term stewardship.
